About Lee

I grew up on the Big Island of Hawai'i but now reside in Los Angeles, CA. I love coding, surfing, loud music of all types, ramen, stand up comedy, photography, typography, graphic design, craft beer and video games. I have a passion for thoughtful front-end design and presenting information in interesting, meaningful or novel ways. Deciding whether a CSS animation should last .35s or .55s gets me excited!
